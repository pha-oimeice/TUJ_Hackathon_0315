What is HackathonCorp

HackathonCorp is the universe's premier multiversal innovation conglomerate, operating across 1,000 planets and countless parallel dimensions since 2000 B.C. We accelerate breakthrough ideas — from interplanetary logistics to interdimensional consulting — by connecting the finest minds across space, time, and reality. With over 10 trillion employees, we dissolved the box approximately 3,000 years ago.

Our Objectives

  • Pioneer cross-dimensional innovation by funding projects that transcend the limits of known science and conventional commerce.
  • Build an interplanetary workforce representing every civilisation, species, and sentient entity across the known and unknown multiverse.
  • Establish sustainable growth frameworks respecting the natural laws of each dimension — without causing more than three (3) extinction-level disruptions per fiscal cycle.

Our Story

HackathonCorp was founded in 2000 B.C. by visionaries who believed good ideas should not be limited by geography — or physics. What began as a trading post in ancient Mesopotamia expanded beyond the atmosphere when our founders discovered an interdimensional portal in the basement. By the Renaissance, we had consulted on several of history's greatest inventions. Today we operate the largest distributed workforce in the multiverse across 1,000 planets, dozens of timelines, and at least four pocket dimensions legal is still trying to register.

Our Clients

  • Early-stage interplanetary startups seeking prototyping, seed funding, and access to our cross-dimensional mentor network.
  • Galactic enterprises looking to disrupt their own industries before a rival civilisation does it for them.
  • Planetary councils requiring neutral innovation consulting on public infrastructure, defence, or dimensional border policy.
  • Independent inventors and lone geniuses with ideas too large — or too strange — for conventional institutions to accommodate.
